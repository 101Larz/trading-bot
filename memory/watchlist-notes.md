# Watchlist Symbol Notes

Per-symbol thesis and observations. Agent updates these notes as new information emerges.

---

## SPY — S&P 500 ETF

**Role:** Benchmark + macro signal. If SPY is below its 20-day MA, reduce all risk.
**Max allocation:** 15%
**Typical hold period:** Days to weeks (trend following)
**Key watch:** Fed rate decisions, CPI prints, earnings season

---

## QQQ — Nasdaq-100 ETF

**Role:** Tech sector exposure, higher beta than SPY.
**Max allocation:** 10%
**Typical hold period:** Days to weeks
**Key watch:** Moves with NVDA, MSFT, AAPL, META, GOOGL weight changes

---

## NVDA — NVIDIA Corporation

**Role:** High-conviction AI infrastructure play.
**Max allocation:** 5%
**Thesis:** Dominant GPU provider for AI training and inference workloads. Demand driven by hyperscaler capex.
**Risks:** Supply chain, export controls, valuation compression on rate rises
**Key watch:** Data center revenue, export license news, AMD/Intel competitive announcements

---

## AAPL — Apple Inc.

**Role:** Stability anchor, large-cap quality.
**Max allocation:** 5%
**Thesis:** Consistent buyback-driven EPS growth, services revenue expansion.
**Risks:** China supply chain, iPhone upgrade cycle slowdown
**Key watch:** iPhone unit sales, Services revenue, China regulatory news

---

## MSFT — Microsoft Corporation

**Role:** Cloud + enterprise + AI (Azure, Copilot).
**Max allocation:** 5%
**Thesis:** Azure growth + OpenAI integration driving enterprise AI adoption.
**Risks:** Competition from AWS, Google Cloud; regulatory scrutiny
**Key watch:** Azure revenue growth rate, Copilot seat count, LinkedIn performance

---

## AMZN — Amazon.com Inc.

**Role:** E-commerce + AWS cloud diversification.
**Max allocation:** 5%
**Thesis:** AWS re-acceleration + advertising business growth. Margin expansion story.
**Risks:** AWS competition, consumer spending slowdown
**Key watch:** AWS operating income, advertising revenue, operating margin

---

## META — Meta Platforms Inc.

**Role:** Digital advertising + AI infrastructure.
**Max allocation:** 5%
**Thesis:** Ad revenue recovery + Llama/AI investments paying off in engagement.
**Risks:** Regulatory (EU, US), ad market cyclicality, Reality Labs burn rate
**Key watch:** Daily active users, ARPU, AI infrastructure capex announcements

---

## GOOGL — Alphabet Inc.

**Role:** Search monopoly + Cloud + Gemini AI ecosystem.
**Max allocation:** 5%
**Thesis:** Search dominance is defensible; Cloud growing faster than AWS/Azure; Gemini integration.
**Risks:** DOJ antitrust action, search disruption from AI, YouTube competition
**Key watch:** Search revenue, Google Cloud growth, Gemini adoption metrics
